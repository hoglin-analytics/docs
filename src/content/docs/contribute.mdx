We welcome contributions from the community! Whether you're fixing typos,
improving existing content, or adding new guides, your help makes Hoglin
better for everyone.

## Ways to Contribute

- **Fix typos and grammar** - Spot an error? Let us know!
- **Improve clarity** - Help make complex topics easier to understand
- **Add examples** - Real-world use cases and code samples are invaluable
- **Write new guides** - Share your expertise with the community
- **Suggest improvements** - Ideas for better organization or missing topics

## Getting Started

1. **Fork & clone the repository** on [GitHub](https://github.com/hoglin-analytics/docs)
2. **Test your changes** locally if possible
3. **Submit a pull request** with a clear description of your improvements
